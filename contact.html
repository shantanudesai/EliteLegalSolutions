Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our legal services

How much does a legal consultation cost?

+

We offer free initial consultations for most legal matters. During this consultation, we'll assess your case and provide you with information about our services and fees. Detailed fee structures will be discussed based on the complexity and nature of your legal matter.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

+

Please bring any relevant documents related to your legal matter, such as contracts, correspondence, court papers, or other legal documents. Also bring a list of questions you'd like to discuss and any timeline or deadline information that may be relevant to your case.

How long does it take to resolve a legal matter?

+

The timeline varies greatly depending on the type and complexity of your legal matter. Simple document drafting may take a few days, while litigation can take months or years. We'll provide you with a realistic timeline estimate during your consultation based on your specific situation.

Do you handle cases outside of Pune?

+

While our primary office is in Pune, we can handle certain types of legal matters in other jurisdictions within Maharashtra and across India, depending on the nature of the case. We'll discuss the feasibility during your consultation.

Can I get legal advice over the phone or video call?

+

Yes, we offer phone and video consultations for your convenience, especially for initial consultations and follow-up discussions. However, some matters may require in-person meetings for document review and signing.
